# Privacy Policy for Page Saver

**Last Updated: January 1, 2026**

## Overview

Page Saver ("the Extension") is a browser extension that helps users capture screenshots, download web pages, and save images from websites. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles user data.

## Data Collection

**Page Saver does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What we DON'T do:
- ❌ We do not collect any personal information
- ❌ We do not track your browsing history
- ❌ We do not use analytics or telemetry
- ❌ We do not store any data on external servers
- ❌ We do not share any information with third parties
- ❌ We do not use cookies for tracking

### What the Extension does:
- ✅ Captures screenshots of web pages (stored locally on your device)
- ✅ Downloads HTML content from websites (stored locally on your device)
- ✅ Downloads images from websites (stored locally on your device)
- ✅ Stores your last capture information locally in browser storage for convenience

## Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following permissions:

### `activeTab`
- **Purpose**: Access the currently active tab to capture screenshots and extract content
- **Usage**: Only activated when you click the extension icon
- **Data**: No data is collected or transmitted

### `downloads`
- **Purpose**: Save captured screenshots, HTML files, and images to your computer
- **Usage**: Files are saved to your default Downloads folder
- **Data**: Files are stored locally only

### `scripting`
- **Purpose**: Execute scripts on web pages to capture full-page screenshots and extract images
- **Usage**: Scripts run only when you initiate a capture action
- **Data**: No data is collected or transmitted

### `storage`
- **Purpose**: Remember your last successful capture for display in the popup
- **Usage**: Stores only the filename and timestamp of the last capture
- **Data**: Stored locally in your browser, never transmitted

## Data Storage

All data processed by Page Saver remains on your local device:
- Screenshots are saved to your Downloads folder
- HTML archives are saved to your Downloads folder
- Images are saved to your Downloads folder
- Settings and last capture info are stored in local browser storage

## Third-Party Services

Page Saver does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

## Children's Privacy

Page Saver does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.
